Your prompts are stored locally in your browser via Chrome Storage Local. This means that no data is transferred to external servers, so your prompts remain exclusively on your device. Full control: Only you (or your browser profile) can access your stored data. Your data is automatically protected by the browser. Chrome protects your locally stored data using its own security mechanisms, such as isolated browser processes and user rights. There is no risk from API keys or online accounts because PromptIn requires no login, account, or cloud synchronization. Conclusion: Your data is as secure as your device itself. As long as your computer or browser profile is protected, your prompts are protected too — completely offline and without third-party access.
